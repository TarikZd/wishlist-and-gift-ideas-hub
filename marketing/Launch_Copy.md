## Product Hunt Description
**Tagline**: Never forget a gift idea again — your complete wishlist hub in Notion.

**Description**:
Gift giving shouldn't be stressful. The Wishlist & Gift Ideas Hub is a beautifully organized Notion workspace where you can track your own wishlist, manage gift ideas for friends and family, set budgets, and never buy a duplicate gift again.

✨ **Features**:
- **Product Gallery**: Paste image URLs and your wishlist becomes a visual product gallery.
- **Gift Budget Rollups**: See exactly how much you've committed to spend per person via Notion Rollups.
- **Priority Badges**: Formula 2.0 auto-labels items as 🔥 Must Have, ⚡ Would Love, or 🌿 Nice to Have.
- **Separate Views**: Filter by `For = Myself` for your wishlist, or `For = [Name]` to find gift ideas for each person.
- **Plug & Play**: Duplicate and start adding gifts in under 60 seconds.

Give better gifts. Every time.
